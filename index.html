Anastasia Nikitopoulou

Coctail Pianist

Manchester, UK

Bio/Repertoire

Anastasia comes from new Conservatory of Thessaloniki in Greece. She has been played for a large range of events, from weddings and birthday parties to Drinks receptions, local events & ceremonies providing a light and charming background. Participating in various events including the national Piano competition, Anastasia demonstrated a distinctive creativity and natural professionalism and can adapt her large repertoire to the special needs that each occasion requires.

She has played for Hilton Hotel and other various live concerts & local events covering a large variety of repertoire mainly focusing on light jazz, lounge, classical, current pop & hip hop. She is also more than willing to tailor her repertoire to your special requests after having a discussion or a chat in advance.

For contact please fill out the enquiry form.
